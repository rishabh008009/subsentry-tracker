## Tone Examples

### ‚ùå Avoid (Robotic)
- "Error 404: Resource not found"
- "Invalid input detected"
- "Operation completed successfully"
- "Subscription entity created"

### ‚úÖ Use Instead (Human)
- "We couldn't find that page"
- "Please check your entry and try again"
- "All set! Your subscription is saved"
- "We've added your subscription"

---

## Key Principles

1. **Be Supportive**: Celebrate small wins ("Great job!", "All set!")
2. **Be Clear**: Use plain language, avoid jargon
3. **Be Helpful**: Explain what happened and what to do next
4. **Be Empowering**: Focus on user control ("You're all caught up")
5. **Be Human**: Write like you're talking to a friend
